## 🌟 About

**gradeboundaries.com** was built by two A-Level students who wanted a better way to visualise and compare grade boundaries across exam boards. As A-Level students themselves, they understood how valuable it is to spot trends over time — so they built the tool they wished they'd had.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📈 **Interactive Charts** — Visualise grade boundary trends across years at a glance
- 📥 **CSV Downloads** — Export any dataset for your own analysis
- 🔍 **Multi-board Coverage** — Edexcel, CIE, Oxford AQA, and OCR all in one place
- 🆓 **Completely Free** — No login, no paywall, no ads
- 📅 **Up-to-date Data** — Datasets spanning 2015–2026

---

## 📚 Available Datasets

| Exam Board | Coverage | Highlights |
|---|---|---|
| **Edexcel** | 2015 – 2026 | 800+ units across Maths, Sciences, Economics & more |
| **CIE** | 2021 – 2025 | AS & A-Level Cambridge International thresholds |
| **Oxford AQA** | 2018 – 2026 | Full grade boundary data across all subjects |
| **OCR** | 2015 – 2025 | Comprehensive coverage of all major subjects |

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

Visit **[gradeboundaries.com](https://gradeboundaries.com)** and select your exam board from the navigation to instantly browse grade boundary tables and charts.

> ⚠️ JavaScript must be enabled for interactive charts and tables. CSV downloads are still available without it.
